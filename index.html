1.屏幕适配-screenAdapt

(1)调用: MT.screenAdapt(designW);

(2)其中designW为设计稿尺寸，一般为750px/640px;

(3)注：该方法应在初始化时调用;

2.获取url参数-getUrlparam

(1)调用: MT.getUrlparam(href);

(2)href为解析的url，若不传url，则默认解析当前页面地址;

3.获取图片base64编码(图片上传)

(1)调用: MT.getDataUrl(file, callback);

(2)file由change触发，callback为dataUrl的回调;

4.获取url参数-judgePlat

(1)调用: MT.judgePlat();

(2返回weixin/weibo;

5.获取url参数-isAndroid

(1)调用: MT.isAndroid();

(2返回true/false;

6.获取url参数-isIOS

(1)调用: MT.isIOS();

(2返回true/false;
